Our Legacy

A Century of Educational Excellence

Pioneering Education in Bengaluru

Established in 1917, The National College, Basavanagudi was born out of a profound vision to provide nationalist education during India's freedom struggle. Over the past century, the institution has stood as a beacon of intellectual freedom, academic rigor, and cultural heritage.

We believe in nurturing not just scholars, but socially responsible citizens equipped to lead and serve the global community. Our philosophy intertwines traditional values with modern pedagogy.
